Barlow's Wheel Demonstration

This simulation demonstrates the Lorentz force principle where a current-carrying conductor experiences force in a magnetic field.

Physics: F = BIL, where F is force, B is magnetic field, I is current, and L is conductor length.

Adjust parameters to see how they affect rotation speed and direction.
